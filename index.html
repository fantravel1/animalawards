About Our Mars Mission

Help humanity choose which Earth species deserve preservation for our interplanetary future.

How do I vote for animals to go to Mars?

Click the "Vote for Mars" button on any animal card to cast your vote for that species. Each vote helps determine which animals are most deserving of a spot on humanity's journey to Mars.

What criteria determine Mars readiness?

Animals are ranked based on intelligence, adaptability, survival skills, social compatibility, size efficiency, cuteness factor, and overall utility to human colonies on Mars.

How many animals can go to Mars?

Currently, we have allocated 500 ark spots for Earth's most worthy animal species to join the Mars colonization mission.

Can I search for specific animals?

Yes! Use our search function to find animals by name, habitat, or characteristics. You can also filter by category and habitat, or sort by various criteria like intelligence or cuteness.
